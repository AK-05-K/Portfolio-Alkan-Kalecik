About me

Why computer science?

Based in Germany, I enjoy working through problems step by step and continuously refining my approach. Outside of my academic work, I stay active through fitness, particularly football and basketball, enjoy traveling, and value spending time with friends and family as a way to stay balanced.
After graduating from high school in 2022, I was eager to start university right away. Through extensive research, I developed a strong interest in computer science.
As a newcomer to the field, I initially found it challenging to keep up during my first semesters. However, I quickly adapted, improved significantly, and gained confidence in my abilities.
Today, I enjoy what I do and am always looking for new opportunities to grow, improve, and deepen my understanding.
